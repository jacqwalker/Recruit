CV writing tips

  • Use action verbs such as developed, etc
  • Your CV should not read as a job description but rather include key achievements
  • Most hiring managers will review your CV in 30 seconds so make sure the first half page captures their attention
  • Try and keep to 2 and 1/2 pages
  • List qualifications, positions held at the end of the CV, not the begining
  • Have an opening statement - maximum 3 lines summarising your skills and experience to date
