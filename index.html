Who is this for

Qualified ACA, ACCA, CIMA

Walkr jobs is only available to qualified ACA, ACCA and CIMA candidates. As other job boards are unable to screen out candidates without the relevant qualifications, ACA, ACCA and CIMA job seekers may be overlooked by hiring managers due to the overwhelming volume of applications.

Relevant job descriptions

We work with hiring managers to produce the best job descriptions so you have all information available to you when deciding on which job to apply for.

Making the job search easy

Easy search functionality. Direct access to the employing companies. No more external recruiters. You get to decide which jobs are best suited for you. You are in charge.

Our Story

Walkr jobs was started by an ACA; born out of a need to make the job search easier. It is a platform allowing candidates and hiring managers to connect.

Traditional job searches are time consuming and dealing with middlemen can be frustrating on both sides. Our aim is to be the one go to site for qualified ACA, ACCA and CIMA to find their next job, and be the one go to site for companies looking for these candidates.
