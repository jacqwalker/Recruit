Welcome to PQE Careers. This is for all ACAs, ACCAs and CIMA qualified accountants.

Our aim is to help you build and manage your career

Our blog has a wealth of information which will help with your career and is divided into three categories: Corporate Life, Leadership Development and Career Resources. We also offer CV review and coaching services.

In time, we will advertise accounting jobs. We are not a recruitment agency but we know how hard the job search can be. We want to make it easier for you while helping you manage your career

If this is something you would like to be a part of, please sign up below. We ask for your number of years of post-qualified experience so we can tailor roles to your level. In the meantime, we will keep you updated on our progress through our weekly newsletter
