Our services

CV review

Our CV review service is priced at just £45. You send us your CV as it currently stands, and we work together to build a CV that will set you apart. If you’d like to hear more about our CV review services, or if you would like a copy of our free CV template, please do get in touch

Coaching

Whatever your needs, coaching can help address them. Whether you would like to take the next step in your career, feel stuck in a rut and not sure what to do next, or want help on how to deal with a difficult colleague, coaching is for you. We work with some fantastic coaches who have over 10 years’ experience and all sessions are confidential. The first session is free – to give you the opportunity to see how coaching works. If you would like to hear more, please contact jackie@calxx.co.uk
