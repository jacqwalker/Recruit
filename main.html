Why Walkr jobs

Who is this for

Walkr jobs is only available to qualified ACA, ACCA and CIMA candidates. Walkr jobs connects the best candidates with great companies.

Stand out from the crowd

As other job boards are unable to prevent candidates without the relevant qualifications applying for jobs, ACA, ACCA and CIMA job seekers may be overlooked by hiring managers due to the overwhelming volume of applications.

Easy search functionality

The keyword search is a thing of the past, as just by entering number of years PQE, job seekers can quickly identify all available jobs at their level, regardless of job title or industry.

Relevant job descriptions

We work with hiring managers to produce the best job descriptions so you have all information available to you when deciding on which job to apply for.

Direct access

As all applications go directly to the hiring company, neither party has the headache of dealing with external recruiters.

Take charge of your career

You get to choose which jobs are best suited to your strengths. You get to choose which roles you should apply for. You are in control of how you represent yourself to your potential future employer.

Our Story

Walkr jobs was started by an ACA; born out of a need to make the job search easier. It is a platform allowing candidates and hiring managers to connect.

Traditional job searches are time consuming and dealing with middlemen can be frustrating on both sides. Our aim is to be the one go to site for qualified ACA, ACCA and CIMA to find their next job, and be the one go to site for companies looking for these candidates.

Not only are we trying to make the job search process easier, but we also want to help job seekers be at their best. Other services we offer include coaching, CV review services and leadership and development articles that are of interest to this community.
