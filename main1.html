Our Story

I am not sure what will go here

Walkr jobs was started by an ACA born out of a need to make the job search easier and help great candidates find their ideal job.

Traditional job searches are time consuming and candidates do not always have all the facts, or know for sure they are being represented in the way they want to, to potential employers.

With no screening process during the application phase, companies often overlook good candidates due to the sheer number of applications that they receive.

Walkr jobs is only for qualified ACA, ACCA and CIMA candidates and it is our mission to help you find your ideal next role in the easiest way.
